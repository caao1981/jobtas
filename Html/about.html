About Us

JobTask UK LTD - trading as JobTask, established in January 2016 as a Sole Proprietorship and later transitioning to a Private
Limited Company in January 2022, is a global leader in the on-demand service marketplace. Our mission
is simple: to bring essential services to your fingertips with just a few clicks.

Our Journey


Our journey began when our Founder, Kundayi Matambanadzo, operated as a Sole Trader. During this time, he personally
experienced and listened to countless customer complaints about other service providers –
challenges that won't be detailed here for obvious reasons. Recognizing an industry gap and the need for a paradigm shift in service standards, JobTask was born.

Our Commitment to You


At JobTask, we place customer needs above all else. Our commitment is evident from the moment you book a service with
us until its seamless completion or delivery. We understand that excellence is not just a
goal; it's an unwavering commitment.

Why Choose JobTask


  • Unparalleled Convenience: We believe in making your life easier. Our platform allows you to access a wide range of
    services with unparalleled convenience. Whether you need home services like cleaning and
    furniture assembly or on-demand professionals such as mobile barbers, mobile hairdressers, or mobile nail
    technicians and many more, JobTask is your go-to choice.

  • Quality Assurance: Your trust matters to us. We meticulously screen and select every service provider on our platform

  • Customer-Centric Approach: At JobTask, we're all about you, the customer. Our user-centric philosophy guides everything

    ensure you have all the information you need to make informed choices.
